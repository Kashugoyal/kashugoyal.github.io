About Me

Imagine.Innovate.Implement.

My inspiration for pursuing Robotics and Automation stems from the desire to remove drudgery of repititive work, even more so in harsh and hazardous environments. Being in the mining industry, I travelled throughout India and saw the awful fate of the workers and the severe environmental conditions they work in. It is then that I felt the urge to contribute back by developing systems to automate such ardous jobs.

The ventures that I am passionate about are robot assisted farming, autonomous driving, AI. I am a fast learner and possess knowledge and experience in areas such as Manufacturing, Production and Marketing along with Robotics.
