Mecanum Wheel Platform

Jan 2015 - May 2015

Mecanum Wheels are the harbingers of omnidirectional automobiles of the future. Though they have become ubiquitous now, they weren't as popular a few years ago and even less popular in India. I along with my team built this as a project to learn about omni directional manipulation and develop an industrial scale mecanum wheel platfrom capable of carrying loads upto 2 tons.

Being an end to end project, it started with initial CAD modelling followed by material selection, prototyping and then testing. The project was very elaborate and the learnings were tremendous.

The detailed report on the project can be seen here.

The CAD model and renderings can be viewed using this link. All modelling and analysis was done using SolidWorks.
