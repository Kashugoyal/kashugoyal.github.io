Solar Poweres Sterling Engine

Jun 2013 - Jul 2013

Energy from renewable sources is coveted but expensive. Solar cells though highly efficient, involve tremendous amount of capital investment. This makes it difficult for economically weak governments to invest in them. As a cost effective solution to this problem we developed a proof of concept for a sterling engine which uses focused heat energy from the sun's rays.

We collected sun rays using a cocave mirror which was developed using a mosaic of mirrored glass squares pasted on a concave satelite reciever. The focused heat was used to power the engine.
